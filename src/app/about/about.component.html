About Tyler, our Founder

Tyler, the founder and soul of Garden, has been passionate about plants since he was 7 years old. He began gardening with his grandparents, starting with tomatoes and eventually moving to squash and pumpkin gardening. After a personal spiritual journey, Tyler became a survivalist that can live solely off plants and berries.

Garden is a passion project Tyler has started with encouragement from his shaman. With every 1000th tree sold, Tyler will include a lock of his dreads in a vacuum-sealed package. If you would like to get in contact with Tyler, for business or pleasure, please use the form below.
